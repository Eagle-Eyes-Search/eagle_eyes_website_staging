Demos

Scroll horizontally below to see all demos, or Click here to view the album and play the video demos. Best viewed in horizontal orientation if on mobile.
App Intro: In this live demo, we show how the Eagle Eyes App can be used to locate a person, save the location and images, and send a message with the coordinates of the detection.

EagleEyes Walkthrough: This 8 minute walkthough demonstrates how to use EagleEyes in the field, from entering initial search coordinates, to automatically detecting the subject with the drone, to using the map to locate the subject on foot.

App Intro 2: Another demonstration of the app being used to scan through a forest. This one shows some of the false-positives that may come up, due to brightly coloured leaves or flowers.

Image-warping on live video: This is a screen recording from an early version EagleEyes app while flying the drone in New Mexico. In this demo, EagleEyes uses pixel-warping to enhance a hard-to-see person.

Object Detection on Prerecorded Video: This is a screen recording from an early version of the EagleEyes app while replaying pre-recorded videos through the detection system. We can see that in certain settings, the app can detect small objects better than a human would.

Object Detection and Anomolous Colour Spreading: In this early concept demo, we show two options for bringing objects to the attention of the operator.
Static 360 Image Analysis: In this early concept demo, we show how EagleEyes can help quickly detect small objects in a 360 image.
