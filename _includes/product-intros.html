Eagle Eyes Pilot for Android

All-in-one solution for SAR drone pilots

A DJI drone piloting app that runs directly on the controller or on any Android device on a supported headless controller. Comes with anomaly detection and CalTopo integration. Now in beta (launching soon), supporting select DJI drones.

  • Standalone piloting app for select DJI drones (switch to other apps in-flight)
  • Live-track your drone in a CalTopo map
  • Upload images and their coordinates to CalTopo as photo waypoints
  • See color and motion anomalies live on your controller display
  • Can be used alongside Eagle Eyes Scan in-flight for deeper anomaly analysis
