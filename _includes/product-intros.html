Eagle Eyes Pilot for Android

All-in-one solution for SAR drone pilots

A DJI drone piloting app that runs directly on the controller or on any Android device on a supported headless controller. Comes with anomaly detection and CalTopo integration. Now in beta (launching soon), supporting select DJI drones.

  • Standalone piloting app
  • Live-track your drone in CalTopo
  • Upload images to CalTopo as photo waypoints
  • See color and motion anomalies live on your display
  • Can be used alongside Eagle Eyes Scan for advanced inspection

Eagle Eyes Scan for desktop

In-depth analysis of aerial footage on large displays

A desktop application that can be used to locate and inspect objects of interest in aerial footage. Its simple interface helps the operator to quickly investigate images and video, both post-flight and live from the drone, and easily share findings with their team.

  • Scan live drone feeds for color and motion anomalies
  • Review live or recorded footage (zoom, pause, replay)
  • Filter anomalies by color uniqueness or proximity
  • View anomalies and drone paths on interactive maps
  • Process large datasets quickly—online or offline
