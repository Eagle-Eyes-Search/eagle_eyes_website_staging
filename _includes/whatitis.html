What it is

EagleEyes is an Android (and future iOS) application for piloting DJI drones. It is being designed to help Search and Rescue teams locate missing persons. In addition to normal drone-piloting functions, EagleEyes can enhance the live video stream to bring small but potentially important objects to the attention of the operator. It does this by looking for colours and motion in a scene that stand out from the background. This can help pick out objects that a human operator could easily miss otherwise.

Scroll horizontally to view different images. Tap "Detections" or "Warp" to see the different ways that EagleEyes can bring interesting objects to the attention of the operator. Best viewed horizontally on mobile.
