What it is

EagleEyes is an android application for piloting DJI drones. It is being designed to help Search and Rescue teams locate missing persons. In addition to normal drone-piloting functions, the App can enhance the live video stream to bring small but potentially important objects to the attention of the operator.

It does this by looking for anomolous colours in a scene and "inflating" their pixels to make them jump out to the operator.

Move the slider to the left to see the raw image enhanced by EagleEyes (best viewed in horizontal orientation if on mobile)
