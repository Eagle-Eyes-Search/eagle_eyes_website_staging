Eagle Eyes is a computer vision system that helps Search and Rescue teams locate missing persons by drone.


Eagle Eyes Pilot is an Android App with a built-in computer vision system, for piloting DJI drones. The system scans the live video stream for anomalies, brings any detections to the attention of the operator, and records their coordinates.


Eagle Eyes Scan is a desktop app for MacOS/Windows, for scanning through video post-flight. It provides a simple, intuitive interface enabling the operator to work alongside the computer vision system to scan the video in detail.
