Eagle Eyes Pilot

Eagle Eyes Pilot is an Android App with a built-in computer vision system, for piloting DJI drones. The system scans the live video stream for anomalies, brings any detections to the attention of the operator, and records their coordinates the map. The app enables the operator to easily switch between visual and thermal camera feeds, and records a database of detections for review.

Eagle Eyes Scan

Eagle Eyes Scan is a desktop app for scanning through pre-recorded drone footage for anomalies. It enables the operator to scan through video, zoom, pan, switch to thermal, and review detections with ease. Eagle Eyes scan is currently available for MacOS. Windows and Linux versions are in the works.


Eagle Eyes is under development. As of February 2023, we are preparing the first prototype for deployment.
