Eagle Eyes Pilot Setup

Get started with Eagle Eyes Pilot in 4 simple steps

  1. 1
    Download Eagle Eyes Pilot
    Go to the download page and get the APK file for your Android device or DJI controller. Make sure you have at least 6 GB of free space on your device.
  2. 2
    Install the APK
    Install the downloaded .apk on your device. If it's blocked, enable "Install from Unknown Sources" in your settings. This is typically found under Settings → Security or Settings → Applications.
  3. 3
    Activate your license
    Open Eagle Eyes Pilot. When asked for location access, set it to "While using the app".
    In the main menu, tap the button next to "No License. Get one here →" and follow the steps to activate your license.
  4. 4
    Configure Your Device and start flying!
    Setup your controller via "..." → Setup Controller.
    Once you connect your controller, you are ready to fly!
