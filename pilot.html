Eagle Eyes Pilot

An Android App with a built-in computer vision system, for piloting DJI drones.

The system scans the live video stream for anomalies, bringing any detections to the attention of the operator, while recording their coordinates.

Find more info in the Eagle Eyes Scan User Manual.
