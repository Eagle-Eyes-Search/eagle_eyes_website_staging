Eagle Eyes Pilot is an Android App with a built-in computer vision system, for piloting DJI drones. The system scans the live video stream for anomalies, bringing any detections to the attention of the operator. The system integrates smoothly with CalTopo, allowing you to import your CalTopo map objects into Eagle Eyes Pilot, livetrack the drone into CalTopo, and drop detections into your CalTopo map.
