Frequently Asked Questions

  • What hardware requirements are needed for Eagle Eyes?

    Eagle Eyes is designed to run on modern systems with at least 8GB RAM and an i5 processor or equivalent. For optimal performance with large datasets, we recommend 16GB RAM and an i7 processor or better.

  • Do you offer multi-user licenses?

    Yes, we offer various licensing options including multi-user and enterprise licenses. Please visit our pricing page or contact us for more details.

  • Is training included with purchase?

    Basic training materials are included with all purchases. For enterprise solutions, we offer comprehensive training sessions tailored to your team's needs.

  • How often are updates released?

    We typically release major updates quarterly and minor updates monthly. All updates are included with active licenses.

  • Can Eagle Eyes integrate with our existing systems?

    Yes, our enterprise solutions offer API integrations that can connect with your existing systems. Our team can work with you to ensure smooth integration.
