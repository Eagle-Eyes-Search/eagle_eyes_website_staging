Eagle Eyes Scan is a desktop app for MacOS/Windows. The app helps Search and Rescue teams scan through aerial imagery both post-flight and as it is being live-streamed from the drone. It comes with an automatic detection system that can identify objects of interest in a search, and reporting tools to help you document your findings and send them to the ground team.

Find more info in the Eagle Eyes Scan User Manual, or by talking to EagleEyesGPT, our AI Chatbot.
