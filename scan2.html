Eagle Eyes Scan is a desktop app for Search and Rescue teams to locate missing persons by drone.


Real-time detection for any drone

Use on any drone with a livestream, or scan prerecorded video or images.

Find color and motion anomalies

Identify objects as small as a pixel, unnoticed by the human eye.

Fast and simple

Scan live video or large datasets with minimal training or tuning.


Experience our Automatic Detection System in action

Download Eagle Eyes Scan

Eagle Eyes Scan comes standard in a feature-limited Free tier with a sample library that showcases real-time object detection to locate people in aerial videos and images. A SAR license is required to use the system using your own footage or live-streamed video.
