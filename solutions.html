Eagle Eyes Solutions

Comprehensive drone search and rescue solutions powered by advanced technology and real-time integration.

Pro Features & Solutions

Our Pro plan includes access to all Eagle Eyes products and features for 1 year. Here are the key solutions available:

  • Upload drone GPS tracks to CalTopo
    Fly online or offline in Eagle Eyes Pilot or DJI Pilot 2, then quickly upload your drone's GPS tracks to CalTopo once you're back in service.
  • Convert CalTopo search areas into DJI flight mission plans
    Easily turn CalTopo search areas into KML files and import them directly into DJI Pilot 2 as ready-to-fly mission plans.
  • AirOps map view with live multi-drone tracking
    When connected to the same local Wi-Fi network as drone controllers broadcasting their locations to the Eagle Eyes AirOps map (while flying in either Eagle Eyes Pilot or DJI Pilot 2), view the AirOps map and see near-zero latency live multi-drone positions overlaid on CalTopo map features.
  • Fly your drone with Eagle Eyes Pilot app
    Use Eagle Eyes Pilot as a complete piloting app with advanced flight controls and a map view that allows you to fly with reference to your CalTopo map.
  • Full access to Eagle Eyes computer vision
    Computer vision color and motion anomaly detection, both real time on the live feed while the drone is in the air and for post processing analysis of recorded images and videos.
  • Real-time CalTopo live location / GPS tracking
    Stream your drone's live GPS location and GPS tracks directly into CalTopo while flying in either Eagle Eyes Pilot or DJI Pilot 2.
  • Add full res geolocated photos to CalTopo while the drone is in the air
    Upload up to full-resolution geolocated drone images to CalTopo as photo points.
  • Full access to all Eagle Eyes Scan (desktop app) features
    Access the full Eagle Eyes Scan feature set, including:
    • Real-time detection on live drone imagery via clean HDMI feed
    • Real-time coordinate transmission from DJI drone controllers over local Wi-Fi networks to display drones live location on the Eagle Eyes Scan map
    • Compatibility with any drone capable of clean HDMI output
    • Ability to run the detector in real-time while flying in Eagle Eyes Pilot or DJI Pilot 2 and feeding an HDMI livestream into Eagle Eyes Scan from your drone controller
    • Post-mission computer vision analysis of still images and video files
    • Mosaic view for fast review of large image sets and videos, highlighting anomalies and geolocating detections
    • Export detections as geolocated photo points into CalTopo or other mapping tools
